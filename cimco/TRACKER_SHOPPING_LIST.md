## EXACT PRODUCT RECOMMENDATIONS

### Best ESP32 Choice: ESP32-C3 Super Mini
**Why**: 
- Tiny size (fits better in cases)
- Native USB (easier programming)
- Low power consumption
- WiFi + Bluetooth
- ~$2.50 each in bulk

**Amazon**: Search "HiLetgo ESP32-C3 Super Mini"  
**AliExpress**: Search "ESP32-C3-MINI-1-N4" 

### Best GPS: NEO-6M with Ceramic Antenna
**Why**:
- Cheap ($5-8)
- Good accuracy (2.5m)
- Works with scrap yard obstructions
- UART interface (easy)

**Amazon**: "Gowoops NEO-6M GPS Module"  
**AliExpress**: "NEO-6M GPS Module with Antenna"

### Best Accelerometer: MPU6050
**Why**:
- Perfect for vibration detection
- I2C interface (2 wires!)
- Built-in motion processor
- Industry standard

**Amazon**: "HiLetgo MPU-6050 3 Axis"  
**AliExpress**: "MPU6050 GY-521 Module"

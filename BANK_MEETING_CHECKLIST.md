## üéØ WHAT TO ASK THE BANK

### Questions to Ask:
1. **"What's the monthly fee for the business checking account?"**
   - Look for: Free or low-fee account ($0-15/month)

2. **"How many free transactions do I get per month?"**
   - Look for: Unlimited or 100+ transactions

3. **"Do you offer business credit cards? What's the process?"**
   - Good for: Building business credit, separating expenses

4. **"Can I integrate online payment processors like Stripe?"**
   - Should be yes - standard ACH transfers

5. **"Do you have a business banker I can work with as we grow?"**
   - Relationship matters - you may need a line of credit later

6. **"What's required to add an employee to the account in the future?"**
   - Planning ahead for when you hire

## ðŸŽ¯ PRO TIPS

### For Phone Recording:
- **Hold phone VERTICAL** (portrait mode) for natural mobile demo
- **Turn OFF notifications** (Do Not Disturb mode) before recording
- **Close other apps** to improve performance
- **Use good lighting** if showing your face
- **Speak clearly** - built-in mic works fine

### For Computer Recording:
- **Close unnecessary tabs/programs** before recording
- **Use Chrome Developer Tools** (F12) to simulate mobile view
- **Set browser window to 50-75% screen size** for better visuals
- **Turn OFF desktop notifications** (Windows: Focus Assist, Mac: Do Not Disturb)
- **Test audio levels** - speak from 1-2 feet from laptop mic

### General Tips:
- **Practice once** before final recording (takes 5 min)
- **Keep it under 3 minutes** - management attention span!
- **Don't worry about perfection** - a few stumbles are fine
- **Show, don't tell** - let the app features speak for themselves
- **End with the cost** - "Under $700 total, ready Monday"
